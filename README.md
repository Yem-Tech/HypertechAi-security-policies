## 📄 Document Summaries

### 1. Privacy Management Policy
**Last Updated:** February 2026

Establishes a comprehensive framework for protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal and sensitive data processed by HypertechAI. Key areas covered include:

- Data collection, storage, use, disclosure, and disposal procedures
- Data subject rights (correction requests, right to be forgotten)
- Consent management and data processing authorizations
- Vendor and third-party data sharing controls
- Privacy impact assessments and ongoing monitoring
- A structured control set (PRV-01 through PRV-18) aligned to industry best practices

**Applies to:** All employees, contractors, vendors, and stakeholders.

---

### 2. Password Construction Standard
**Last Updated:** April 2025

Provides best practices for creating strong passwords across all HypertechAI systems and accounts. Key requirements include:

- Minimum password length of **16 characters** for all work-related accounts
- Encouragement of **passphrases** (e.g., multi-word combinations) for memorability and strength
- Coverage across all account types: user-level, system-level, web, email, voicemail, and router logins
- Periodic password auditing by the Information Security (Infosec) Team
- Sanctions for non-compliance consistent with HR procedures

**Applies to:** All employees, contractors, consultants, and third-party personnel.

---

### 3. Acceptable Use Standard
**Last Updated:** April 2025

Outlines the rules and expectations for appropriate use of HypertechAI's technology resources, networks, and electronic devices. Key areas covered include:

- General use and ownership of company assets
- Security and proprietary information handling
- Prohibited system and network activities (e.g., unauthorized access, port scanning, malware introduction)
- Email and communication conduct
- Blogging and social media guidelines
- Sanctions for policy violations

**Applies to:** All employees, contractors, consultants, temporary workers, and third-party affiliates using HypertechAI-owned or leased equipment.

## 🏗️ How to Use This Repository

1. **For learning/portfolio purposes** — Browse the documents to understand how real-world cybersecurity policies are structured.
2. **For adaptation** — Use these as templates when building out a GRC program. Replace HypertechAI with your organization's name and tailor each section to your environment.
3. **For compliance mapping** — The Privacy Management Policy's PRV control set can be mapped against frameworks such as NIST CSF, ISO 27001, or SOC 2.

## 🛡️ Policy Enforcement

All three documents include a consistent enforcement clause: non-compliance may result in disciplinary action ranging from mandatory retraining and written warnings up to termination of employment or legal consequences, depending on the severity of the violation.
